About

Founded in 2018 by childhood friends and food enthusiasts, Hossam and Mahmoud, the vision was to create a welcoming space where people could enjoy high-quality, locally-sourced dishes. With Hossam's expertise in gourmet cooking and Mahmoud's flair for hospitality, they transformed a quaint, historic building into a vibrant eatery. Today, our restaurant stands as a testament to their hard work and dedication, offering a unique dining experience that celebrates both tradition and innovation.
